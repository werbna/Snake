How to Play Snake

Use the arrow keys to move the snake:

  • Up: ArrowUp
  • Down: ArrowDown
  • Left: ArrowLeft
  • Right: ArrowRight

Eat the fruit to grow the snake. If the snake runs into the wall or itself, Get Good.

Fill the entire board with the snake to win the game!
